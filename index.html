// ABOUT J-POP

What is J-POP?

J-pop, short for Japanese Pop, refers to modern popular music from Japan. It is far more than just a musical genre; it is a cultural phenomenon that seamlessly blends rock, electronic, hip-hop, R&B, and other diverse elements into a unique musical aesthetic.

From the golden era of 80s City Pop to contemporary next-gen artists like YOASOBI and King Gnu, J-pop continues to evolve, using music to tell compelling stories.
